Blooming Fools (BFH3) is a noncompetitive running social club (kennel) of the international Hash House Harriers. Runners and walkers (adults 21 and over) are welcome to join us on a 2- to 5-mile trail (hash) around Bloomington, Indiana:

  • Every other Saturday (4 PM in summer, 3 PM in winter)
  • Full moon evenings (7 PM)

What to expect

On trail: You will follow marks laid in flour leading you to beer, non-alcoholic drinks, and snacks. Along the way, you will face obstacles (shiggy), such as:

  • hills and mud
  • weeds and brush
  • water and road crossings
  • wildlife and wild crowds
  • alleys and tunnels
  • sculptures and playgrounds

After trail: We celebrate with refreshments while singing silly and bawdy songs.

Cost: Your first hash is free. Afterwards, it costs $7 per hash (or $20 for 3).

One of us: If you hash with us several times, we may bestow upon you a special name that you can be known as within the wider hashing community.

You should bring: a sense of humor, an open mind, a whistle, old shoes, leg protection (long socks or pants), bug repellent, a headlamp, and a reusable drinking vessel.

You should not bring: pets (some dogs may be allowed with permission), children, items you are not willing to carry, and drugs.

Bonus items: Bring extra cash if you want to purchase special swag (haberdashery), such as: hats, shirts, patches, and stickers.
